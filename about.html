About VG CS Classes

Building the future of technology education, one student at a time. Discover our journey towards excellence in computer science coaching.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, VG CS Classes emerged from a simple vision: to make quality computer science education accessible to every student in Sikar. What started as a small coaching center has now grown into the region's most trusted computer science institute.

Our founder, with years of industry experience in top tech companies, understood the gap between academic curriculum and industry requirements. This understanding shaped our teaching methodology that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Today, we're proud to have guided over 1500 students towards successful careers in technology, with many placed in top companies and prestigious institutions across India.

Our Journey

Milestones that mark our path to excellence

2019

Foundation

VG CS Classes was founded with a vision to provide quality computer science education in Sikar. Started with just 20 students and basic facilities.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adapted to online learning during the pandemic, introduced digital classrooms and remote learning capabilities. Student count grew to 150.

2021

First Success Stories

Our first batch of competitive programming students achieved remarkable success in coding competitions. Infrastructure expanded with new labs.

2022

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with leading tech companies for placements and internships. Launched advanced competitive programming batches.

2023

Award Recognition

Received "Best Computer Science Institute in Sikar" award. Student count crossed 1000 with 95% success rate in placements.

2024

Present Day

Leading computer science institute in Rajasthan with 1500+ students, modern infrastructure, and proven track record of success.
